Our Services

What We Offer

Residential Cleaning

We provide a whole house detail-cleaning. We'll carefully detail-clean your kitchen, bathrooms, living areas and sleeping areas while providing a thorough cleaning to the rest of your house. All supplies are provided.

Commercial Cleaning

We have a low office cleaning rate that makes commercial cleaning service affordable for companies of all sizes. Our office cleaning services are specifically tailored to suit your unique requirements and we work as per your schedule. We help provide a clean, healthy environment for you and your employees that is done to your complete satisfaction. All supplies are provided.

Post Construction / After Renovation Clean-up

Leave the dirty work to a professional cleaning service that will take as much pride in your house or facility as you do. Morales Cleaning Service LLC specializes in post construction and after renovation cleaning. We assist in the process of removing all traces of construction dirt and dust from new and remodeled structures. All supplies are provided.

After death / One-time Cleaning

After death and one-time cleaning consists of a thorough, overall cleaning that includes basic cleaning plus clean and dust all baseboards, dust all wall hangings, clean and dust window sills, clean interior windows and dust light fixtures. All supplies are provided.

Additional Services

In addition to the standard cleaning services your home or business may require, Morales Cleaning Service LLC offers additional services to keep your home or workplace looking its best. These services can be added to your invoice for an additional fee upon request at the time of your estimate. These additional services include but not limited to cleaning blinds, detail cleaning of microwave, refrigerators, hand washing of tile or wood floors, detail cleaning of the laundry room and washing the walls.
